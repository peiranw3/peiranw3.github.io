Sleep Health and Lifestyle Narrative Visualization

Introduction

Sleep is a fundamental aspect of human health and well-being, influencing everything from cognitive function to physical health. Adequate sleep is essential for maintaining critical bodily functions, supporting mental health, enhancing productivity, and improving overall quality of life.

This narrative visualization explores the relationship between physical activity, mental health, resting heart rate, and sleep quality using data from the Sleep Health and Lifestyle Dataset. By examining these connections, we aim to shed light on how different aspects of our daily lives can impact the quantity of our sleep, and how it can affect our health.
